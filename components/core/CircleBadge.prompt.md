Engagement-circle identity pill — the HEART analytic spine. Use anywhere a segment, quote, or metric is attributed to a circle.

```jsx
<CircleBadge circle={1} />               {/* Community Voice — clay */}
<CircleBadge circle={3} size="sm" />     {/* Leadership — deep blue */}
<CircleBadge circle={2} showLabel={false} />
```

`circle`: 1 Community Voice, 2 Internal Stakeholders, 3 Leadership. Colors are fixed by the circle token system.
